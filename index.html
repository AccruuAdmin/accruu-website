Designed for Real Life

Real Investments, Not Points

Every micro-investment buys actual fractional ETF shares through an SEC-registered broker-dealer. This is your money, in your brokerage account, growing in the real market.

Goal-Based Onboarding

Tell us your screen time reduction goal and timeline. Accruu generates a personalized roadmap with milestones, tracking your progress from day one.
